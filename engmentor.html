About Our Engineering Mentors

'Females with Incredible skills'

We can provide your school, college, university or workplace with mentors from a diverse range of engineering backgrounds from around the globe


They bring with them a wealth of knowledge and are role models in their own rights that women can aspire too. They are trained to provide mentorship to all ages and all have personal reasons and goals to help women suceed getting into and flourishing in Engineering.
